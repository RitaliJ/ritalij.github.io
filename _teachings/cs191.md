I supported a course on biomolecular computation that explores how molecular systems can encode information, perform computation, and generate organized behavior. ### Course Focus
- Molecular information processing and computation
- Self-assembly and pattern formation
- Theoretical models of reaction dynamics and emergent behavior

### My Role

- Supported problem-solving and technical discussion during office hours
