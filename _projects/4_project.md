Implemented a Qwen2 large-language-model inference engine on GPU for autoregressive single-token decoding. The work included custom CUDA kernels for LayerNorm, linear layers, SwiGLU activation, rotary positional embeddings, and grouped-query attention, along with optimization of key/value cache handling. Key technologies: CUDA, transformer inference, and ML systems engineering.
