Designed a modular policy framework for food delivery assignment under time-varying demand, driver availability, and routing constraints. The project framed the system as a dynamic matching and routing problem and compared policies for stability, adaptability, and delivery performance. Key technologies: Optimization, transportation modeling, and systems design.
