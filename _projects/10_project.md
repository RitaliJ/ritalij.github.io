Designed and built a light-reactive robot car using a Raspberry Pi Pico, photoresistors, an L298N H-bridge, and DC motors. The system used analog light sensing and PWM-based motor control to track and follow a flashlight beam, combining embedded programming, circuit integration, and mechanical assembly into a functional autonomous robot. Key technologies: Raspberry Pi Pico, PWM, photoresistors, DC motors, and embedded control.
